About Us

Our story

Founded in 2024, alQa Brands started as a two‑person design studio in Nairobi. Today we are a full‑service creative agency and print house, trusted by over 500 businesses across Kenya. We blend strategic thinking with bold creativity to help brands stand out—from logo design to large‑scale signage.

📍 Based in Nairobi, serving the world. Our in‑house production facility allows us to control quality every step of the way, ensuring fast turnaround and exceptional results.
